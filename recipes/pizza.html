This pizza is perfect for a nice chill at home party on a friday.

The classic pepperoni pizza works any day at any mood.

The recipe can be simple and advanced, based on your preference. The most time consuming part is preparing the dough. The resting time can vary from an hour to 48 hours. Which makes the recipe either a simple pizza or an advanced cooking experience.

Ingredients

Dough
  • Flour, 10dl
  • Water, 5dl
  • Olive Oil, 2 table spoons
  • sugar, 1 table spoon
  • Yeast
Sauce
  • Crushed tomatoes, 500g
  • Garlic, 3 cloves
  • Oregano, 1 tea spoon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
Toppings
  • Mushrooms
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Ham
  • Olives

Steps

Making the dough
  1. Start by mixing water and yeast in a bowl. The water's temperature should be body temperature if you want the dough to be ready faster, otherwise it can be cold water.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and start mixing the dough. Start kneeding and add flour little by little when it's too sticky.
  3. After the dough has a smooth surface or about 10 minutes, it should be finished. Put it aside in the bowl under a towel
Making the sauce
  1. Mix all of the ingredients. Now you have your sauce.
Making the pizza
  1. After the dough has rested for at least 45 minutes, you can divide it into 3-4 dough balls.
  2. Shape them and roll them tightly, and let them rest for about 30 minutes.
  3. Take on dough ball and make it flat and slowly stretch it out from the middle to the edge.
  4. When the dough is stretched as big as you want it, add your toppings and put it in the oven for about 10 minutes.
